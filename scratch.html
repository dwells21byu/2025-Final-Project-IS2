My Love for Tennis & Exploring the World

Tennis is one of my favorite hobbies—especially doubles on hard courts. Federer has always been my favorite player because of his effortless style and sportsmanship. When I’m not playing tennis, I love traveling the world.

I’ve visited Morocco, Cape Verde, Italy, France, England, Ireland, Portugal, Canada, and Mexico. Every country brings new experiences, food, people, and culture. Check out the Tableau at the bottom of the page for more information about safe places you can travel!

How I Improve My Tennis Game

My monthly improvement routine:

  1. Footwork & Movement
    • Split-step timing.
    • Fast recovery to center court.
  2. Doubles Strategy
    • Net presence & poaching.
    • Strong communication.
  3. Shot Selection
    • High-percentage targets.
    • Avoiding unnecessary pace.
